Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get ZemiMod up and running:

  1. Download both ZemiMod.dll and ZemiInjector.zip.
  2. Extract the mod archive anywhere on your PC.
  3. Run ZemiInjector.exe.
  4. Select the Rocket League process and inject the mod.
  5. Launch Rocket League and enjoy the mod features!
