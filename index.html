Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get ZemiMod up and running:

  1. Download the ZemiMod.zip file.
  2. Extract the archive anywhere on your PC.
  3. Run ZemiInjector.exe from the extracted files.
  4. Select the Rocket League process and inject the mod.
  5. Launch Rocket League and enjoy the mod features by pressing the insert key!
